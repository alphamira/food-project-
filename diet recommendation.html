General tips for a healthy diet:

  • Eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. These foods are low in calories and packed with nutrients, including vitamins, minerals, and fiber. Aim for at least five servings of fruits and vegetables per day.
  • Limit unhealthy fats. Saturated and trans fats can increase your risk of heart disease, stroke, and other chronic health problems. Choose unsaturated fats, such as those found in olive oil and avocados, instead.
  • Choose lean protein sources. Lean protein, such as fish, poultry, beans, and lentils, can help you feel full and satisfied without adding too many calories to your diet.
